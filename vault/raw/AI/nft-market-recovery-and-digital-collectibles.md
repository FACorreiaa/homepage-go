The NFT market is showing signs of recovery as crypto markets strengthen and collector interest returns. Key trends include:

**Bitcoin NFTs:** The emergence of Bitcoin-based NFTs through protocols like Ordinals has created a new market segment. Bitcoin NFTs are attracting collectors who want to diversify their holdings beyond Ethereum-based digital assets.

**Blue-Chip Collections:** Established NFT collections from the 2021 bull run are regaining value as crypto wealth increases. Projects like Bored Ape Yacht Club, CryptoPunks, and Art Blocks are seeing renewed trading activity and community engagement.

**Utility Integration:** NFTs are increasingly being integrated with real-world utility, including membership access, event ticketing, and digital identity verification. This shift from pure speculation to utility-driven value is attracting more sustainable collector interest.

**Gaming and Metaverse:** Blockchain-based games and virtual world projects are incorporating NFTs as playable assets, creating new use cases for digital ownership in interactive environments.

The overall recovery in crypto markets, with Bitcoin up 29.7% since the US-Iran conflict began, has provided a favorable backdrop for NFT market revitalization. As institutional adoption grows through crypto investment products, NFTs may benefit from increased mainstream attention and capital inflows.
