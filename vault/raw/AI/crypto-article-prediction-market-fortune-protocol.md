Fortune Protocol is proud to announce the successful completion of its **Seed round financing**, backed by a consortium of leading blockchain and Web3 investors, including **TBV, Cogitent Ventures, X21 Digital, CGV FoF, K24 Ventures, and LandScape Capital**. This milestone marks a pivotal step in the growth of Fortune Protocol as a next-generation **Web3 financial protocol** dedicated to advancing prediction market liquidity infrastructure.

Founded with the vision of redefining decentralized forecasting, Fortune Protocol has rapidly emerged as the **first “fortune cookie”-themed prediction protocol on BSC**, combining engaging user experiences with robust, AI-driven market mechanisms. By providing scalable liquidity infrastructure for prediction markets, Fortune empowers participants to make informed, data-driven decisions while creating a vibrant, transparent, and efficient ecosystem.

Since its launch, **Fortune Dapp has demonstrated extraordinary growth**. According to **BSC Chain Dapp Bay**, the Dapp’s **monthly transaction volume surged by 1287.88%**, with **total transaction volume exceeding 2.14M** and **registered users reaching 119.33K**, securing **#1 position on the New Listing leaderboard**.

**Source:**[https://dappbay.bnbchain.org/detail/fortune](https://dappbay.bnbchain.org/detail/fortune)

The funds raised in this Seed round will be strategically allocated to accelerate the development of **Fortune’s AI-powered prediction tools and large-scale prediction models**, enabling more precise and reliable market forecasts. In addition to technology development, the investment will further support **product innovation, ecosystem expansion, and operational growth.**

This strategic financing also underscores the confidence of top-tier investors in Fortune’s vision and long-term potential. By leveraging the expertise and global networks of our investors, Fortune Protocol is well-positioned to expand its footprint in the Web3 prediction market landscape and establish new standards for market efficiency, transparency, and engagement.

With this Seed round completed, Fortune remains committed to its mission: to **build a professional, scalable, and AI-empowered prediction market ecosystem**, bridging the gap between traditional financial insights and decentralized Web3 innovation. We are excited to continue collaborating with our community, investors, and partners to drive the next era of intelligent, data-driven decentralized finance.

Fortune invites the global Web3 community to join us on this journey — as participants, contributors, and co-creators — in shaping the future of **prediction markets powered by AI and decentralized infrastructure**.
