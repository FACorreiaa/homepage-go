**Lending Markets:** Decentralized lending protocols are seeing renewed activity as crypto markets strengthen. With Bitcoin surpassing $80,000 and improved market sentiment, borrowing and lending volumes are increasing across platforms like Aave, Compound, and MakerDAO.

**Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs):** Uniswap and other DEXs continue to dominate the decentralized trading landscape. **Prediction Markets:** Platforms like Polymarket are seeing growing user engagement, with markets covering everything from political events to crypto price movements. This reflects DeFi's expansion beyond basic lending and trading into more sophisticated financial instruments.
