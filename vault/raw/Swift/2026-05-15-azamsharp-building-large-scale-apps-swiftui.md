# Building Large-Scale Apps with SwiftUI: A Guide to Modular Architecture

Link: https://azamsharp.com/2023/02/28/building-large-scale-apps-swiftui.html

Tweet by Mohammad Azam (@azamsharp) promoting his article on how to structure modular architecture for large-scale SwiftUI apps.

## Key Takeaways

- **Problem**: As SwiftUI apps grow, codebases become tangled — views, logic, and data mixed together
- **Solution**: Modular architecture — separate concerns into independent modules
- **Benefits**: Better testability, easier collaboration, faster builds, clearer boundaries
