# Brazilian betting license requirement for App Store availability

**Date:** May 8, 2026
**Source:** [https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=x4eyetnp](https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=x4eyetnp)

Following changes to Brazil's fixed-odds betting regulation, apps with gambling features now require a valid SPA license for distribution on the App Store in Brazil. New app version must be submitted to initiate license verification.
