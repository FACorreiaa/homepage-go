# Apple Developer News - May 2026

## Brazilian betting license requirement for App Store availability

**May 8, 2026**

Following changes to Brazil’s fixed-odds betting regulation, apps with fixed-odds betting (gambling) features can now be distributed on the App Store in Brazil with a valid fixed-odds betting license from the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets (SPA). If your apps include these features (indicated by selecting “Yes” to the gambling question in the age rating questionnaire in App Store Connect), you’ll need to provide this license.

Key requirements:
- Submit a new app version to initiate license verification
- Include license information in App Store Connect's App Review Information section
- Enter license details in the Notes field
- Attach supporting documents

## Now Available: Monthly Subscriptions with a 12-Month Commitment

**April 27, 2026**

New payment option allowing monthly subscriptions with a 12-month commitment on the App Store. This provides more affordable options for subscribers.

Features:
- Subscribers can cancel at any time, preventing renewal after completing their commitment
- Transparent view of completed and remaining payments in Apple Account
- Email and push notifications remind subscribers of upcoming renewals
- Available worldwide (except US and Singapore) on iOS 26.4+, iPadOS 26.4+, macOS Tahoe 26.4+, tvOS 26.4+, visionOS 26.4+
- Requires iOS 26.5+ for full availability starting May 2026

## App Store expands support to 11 new languages

**March 31, 2026**

App Store Connect now supports localized metadata for 11 new languages, bringing total to 50. New languages: Bangla, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Odia, Punjabi, Slovenian, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu.

Benefits:
- Localize app name, description, screenshots, and more
- Reach more users across languages and cultures
- Grow your business in new markets
- Use new localized App Store badges in marketing

## Updated Apple Developer Program License Agreement now available

**March 30, 2026**

Revised license agreement to support new features, updated policies, and clarifications. Key changes:

- Foveated Streaming framework: specified requirements and data privacy
- Family Controls framework: specified requirements
- Accessory Notifications framework and Accessory Live Activities framework: specified requirements

Translations will be available within one month.

## Update on regulated medical device apps

**March 26, 2026**

App Store will now display whether an app is a regulated medical device on its product page in EEA, UK, and US.

Requirements for apps that:
- Have primary/secondary category Health & Fitness or Medical
- Contain frequent references to Medical or Treatment Information

App Store will display regulated medical device status to provide transparency to customers.
