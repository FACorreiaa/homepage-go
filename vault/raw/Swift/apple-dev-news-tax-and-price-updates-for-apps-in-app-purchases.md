# Tax and price updates for apps, In-App Purchases, and subscriptions

**Date:** May 11, 2026
**Source:** [https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=y3w8ck7j](https://developer.apple.com/news/?id=y3w8ck7j)

Apple announced App Store price adjustments across 43 currencies and 175 storefronts due to changes in tax regulations and foreign exchange rates. Adjustments are made using publicly available exchange rate information.
