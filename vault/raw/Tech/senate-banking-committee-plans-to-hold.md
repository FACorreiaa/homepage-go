The Senate Banking Committee announced it would hold its markup hearing for the Clarity Act on March 14. This marks a significant step forward in crypto regulatory legislation in the United States.

The Clarity Act aims to provide comprehensive market structure regulations for cryptocurrencies and digital assets. The upcoming hearing will focus on amending and voting on the sweeping crypto legislation, which has been a priority for the industry.
