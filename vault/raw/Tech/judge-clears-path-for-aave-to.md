Judge Margaret Garnett allowed frozen exploit funds on Arbitrum to move to Aave, but the legal freeze follows the assets as terrorism plaintiffs continue their claim.

The court ruling provides a pathway for Aave to recover $71 million in ETH that was previously frozen due to its connection to a North Korea-linked hack. While the decision clears the way for the funds to be moved, the assets remain under a legal freeze as terrorism plaintiffs pursue their claims.

This development highlights the complex legal challenges surrounding stolen cryptocurrency and the difficulties in recovering funds even after court approval.
