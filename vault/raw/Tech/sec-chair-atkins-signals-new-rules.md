SEC Chair Paul Atkins linked the rise of AI-powered financial systems with growing demand for blockchain-based market infrastructure and automated settlement. The chairman indicated that new regulations are being developed to address these emerging technologies.

This signals a shift in the SEC's approach to crypto and blockchain-based financial systems, potentially creating a more favorable regulatory environment for onchain markets and AI-driven finance solutions.
