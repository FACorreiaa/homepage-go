# Texas Bitcoin reserve plans shift from ETF to direct BTC custody

Texas is seeking a custodian to transition its Bitcoin reserve assets from the BlackRock IBIT ETF into direct bitcoin holdings, signaling a preference for self-custody over ETF exposure.
