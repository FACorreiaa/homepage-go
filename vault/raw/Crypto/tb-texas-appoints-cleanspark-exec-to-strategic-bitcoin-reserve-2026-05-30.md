# Texas appoints CleanSpark exec to Strategic Bitcoin Reserve committee

Texas is transitioning its Bitcoin reserve from the BlackRock IBIT ETF to direct bitcoin custody, appointing a CleanSpark executive and a bitcoin miner CEO to the oversight committee.
