# Uniswap DAO votes on Proposal 96: UNI burn expansion to BNB Chain, Polygon, Celo

**Source:** [DEXTools](https://www.dextools.io/news/uniswap-uni-burn-expansion-bnb-polygon-celo-vote-2026)  
**Published:** May 26, 2026  
**Ingested:** 2026-06-02T10:12:01Z

Uniswap DAO is voting on Proposal 96 to extend protocol fees and UNI token burning to BNB Chain, Polygon, and Celo.

**Details:**
- If approved: 13 chains total burning UNI (10 current + 3 new)
- Current chains: Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, OP Mainnet, Soneium, X Layer, Worldchain, Zora, Unichain, Ink
- New: BNB Chain (Wormhole NTT), Polygon (Wormhole NTT), Celo (OP Stack)
- Snapshot temperature check passed strongly supportive May 21
- Protocol fee: 1/5 of LP tier
- Burn: 0xdead on Ethereum mainnet
- UNI supply: 1 billion fixed cap

**Impact:** Converts UNI from governance-only token to one with onchain-verifiable buyback program tied to protocol revenue.
