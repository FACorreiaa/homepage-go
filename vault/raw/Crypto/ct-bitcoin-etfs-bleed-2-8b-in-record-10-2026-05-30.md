# Bitcoin ETFs bleed $2.8B in record 10-day outflow streak

Spot Bitcoin ETFs logged a record 10-day outflow streak totaling nearly $3 billion, while Ether ETFs bled for 14 consecutive sessions. Analysts call it a contrarian indicator suggesting a bottom may be near.
