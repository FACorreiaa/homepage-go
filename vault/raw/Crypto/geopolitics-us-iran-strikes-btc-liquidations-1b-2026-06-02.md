# US strikes on Iran trigger $1B in crypto liquidations, BTC drops to $72,840

**Source:** [Phemex](https://phemex.com/blogs/bitcoin-drops-below-73000-us-iran-strikes-crypto-liquidations)  
**Published:** Jun 1, 2026  
**Ingested:** 2026-06-02T10:12:01Z

US strike package targeting Iranian air-defense radar triggered ~$1B in total crypto liquidations across centralized venues.

**Liquidation cascade:**
1. Asian session: Reuters confirmed strikes on Iranian S-300 batteries. BTC $77,200 → $74,800 in 90min. ~$340M longs liquidated.
2. Three hours later: Tehran state TV aired damage footage. BTC $74,000 → $72,840. ~$480M additional liquidations + $180M ETH.

**Recovery:** BTC reclaimed $74,000 after Trump posted Strait of Hormuz blockade "lifting." IRGC retaliated with missile strikes on Kuwait's Ali Al Salem airbase.

**Key levels:** Support $72,800 / $69,400 / $66,800-67,200. Resistance $74,500-75,000 / $77,200.

**Divergence:** Spot BTC ETF flows turned positive (+$38M May 30, +$61M Jun 1) while price fell — historically marks tradeable bottoms.

**Volatility:** One-day realized vol hit 58 (highest since Feb). Deribit 1w ATM implied vol jumped from 38 to 71.
