# Nonsense Bungaku

An original song by Eve, sung by Hatsune Miku, animated by Mah. Eve's self-cover uses the same animated footage and was released on May 20, 2017. Eve included his self-cover MV on his own self-made record label harapeco records's CD+DVD release on December 13, 2017 as a part of his "Bunka" album.
