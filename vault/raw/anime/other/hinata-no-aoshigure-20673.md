# Hinata no Aoshigure

Clumsy and awkward Hinata harbors a crush on his classmate Shigure, and he loves to imagine them on all sorts of fantastical adventures. When Shigure asks Hinata to help her care for the school's birds, he is overjoyed for the opportunity to grow closer to her. However, when a troublesome situation involving Shigure's parents threatens to change Hinata's relationship with her forever, he must find the courage to convey his feelings if he does not want to be forgotten.
