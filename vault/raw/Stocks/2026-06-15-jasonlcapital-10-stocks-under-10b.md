## Full Thread Content

**10 stocks under $10 billion market cap worth keeping an eye on:**

---

### 1. $PENG - Penguin Solutions
**AI-optimized memory solutions and enterprise infrastructure.** Their MemoryAI CXL-based server adds up to 11TB of memory for AI inference workloads. 7 new AI/HPC customer logos in H1. Raised full-year guidance to 12% net sales growth, up from 6%.

---

### 2. $OSS - One Stop Systems
**Builds rugged AI edge computing systems for defense and autonomous applications.** Q1 revenue $8.1M, up 55% YoY. Their Rigel Edge Supercomputer puts data-center-grade AI processing into helicopters and mobile command centers. Guiding for 20-25% revenue growth and positive EBITDA in 2026.

---

### 3. $TE - T1 Energy
**U.S. domestic solar module manufacturer operating a 5GW facility in Dallas.** Q1 revenue $177.7M, up 232% YoY. Just hit their first profitable quarter with $9.1M in adjusted EBITDA. G2 Austin solar cell plant targeting first production Q4 2026 - making them a fully vertically integrated U.S. solar manufacturer.

---

### 4. $UUUU - Energy Fuels
**The largest U.S. uranium producer.** Q1 revenue $35.7M, up 112% YoY. Delivered 510,000 lbs of uranium concentrate at $70.04/lb. Went from zero uranium revenue in Q1 2025 to $35.7M in Q1 2026. Also produces rare earth elements and vanadium from its White Mesa Mill in Utah.

---

### 5. $ONDS - Ondas Holdings
**Autonomous drone systems and wireless infrastructure for defense.** Q1 revenue $50.1M, up over 1,000% YoY. That single quarter roughly equaled all of full-year 2025 revenue. Pro forma backlog exceeds $450M following the Mistral acquisition. Raised FY2026 guidance to $390M+.

---

### 6. $AXTI - AXT Inc
**Manufactures the compound semiconductor substrates used in optical transceivers for AI data centers.** Q1 revenue $26.9M, up 39% YoY. Indium phosphide backlog surpassed $100M - they're doubling InP capacity to meet it. Gross margin swung from negative 6.4% to 29.6% in four quarters.

---

### 7. $KEEL - Keel Infrastructure
**Formerly Bitfarms. Rebranded and redomiciled to the U.S. in April 2026 to pivot from Bitcoin mining to AI data center infrastructure.** 2.2GW power pipeline across Pennsylvania, Washington, and Quebec. $533M in liquidity including $197M in unencumbered Bitcoin. Exited all Latin American operations.

---

### 8. $OUST - Ouster
**Digital lidar sensors for industrial automation, robotics, and smart infrastructure.** Q1 revenue $49M, up 49% YoY. 13 consecutive quarters of product revenue growth. Shipped over 12,600 sensors in Q1 across lidar and camera units. 43% gross margin.

---

### 9. $ADEA - Adeia
**IP licensing company monetizing patents across semiconductors, media delivery, and computing.** Q1 revenue $104.8M, up 20% YoY. New multi-year licensing deals with AMD and Microsoft. Net income nearly doubled YoY to $22.8M. Running a ~60% adjusted EBITDA margin.

---

### 10. $NUAI - New Era Energy & Digital
**Developing AI-focused data center campuses in the Permian Basin.** Texas Critical Data Centers project sits on 438 acres with capacity to support up to 1.4GW of power. Secured a $290M credit facility and raised ~$115M in equity. Earliest stage name on this list - revenue is minimal from legacy gas operations.
