## Platform & Market Rationale

The Hydrochemolytic Technology platform is designed to work across **three verticals**:
- **Plastics** — converting waste plastics to fuel/oil
- **Heavy bitumen** — unlocking stranded heavy oil reserves more efficiently
- **Paraffinic crude** — upgrading lower-quality crude to lighter products

Collectively, these add up to roughly **20–30% of the world's oil supply** — that's the total addressable market (TAM) claim underpinning the thesis.
