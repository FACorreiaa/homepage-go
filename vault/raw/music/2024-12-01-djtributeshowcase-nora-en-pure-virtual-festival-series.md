## About Nora En Pure

**Born:** Daniela Niederer  
**Origin:** South African-Swiss  
**Genre:** Deep House, Indie Dance  
**Label:** Enormous Tunes, Spinnin' Deep

Nora En Pure is celebrated for her uniquely melodic sound, drawing deep inspiration from nature and seamlessly fusing organic instruments — piano, strings — with sophisticated electronic elements. She's widely recognized as a **pioneer in the deep house genre**.

Her career-defining moment came with the 2013 breakout single **"Come With Me"**, which became a global hit and established her reputation for emotionally resonant, uplifting productions. Notable subsequent works include *"Morning Dew"*, *"Lake Arrowhead"*, and *"Conquer Yosemite"* — all reinforcing her standing in deep house.

She's performed at the most prestigious festival stages: **Coachella, Tomorrowland, Ultra Music Festival**. Her live sets are known for their immersive, emotive atmosphere, often described as a sonic journey bridging music and the natural world.

Complementing her live performances, Nora hosts **"Purified Radio"** — her own radio show where she curates tracks from herself and other artists, showcasing her unmistakable taste across the melodic spectrum.
